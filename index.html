About

A Decade & a Half
of Financial Expertise

Gurkirpal Singh Mann is a Chartered Professional Accountant (CPA CGA) with over 18 years of progressive accounting experience across corporate and public practice. Having served as Senior Accountant at Storagevault Canada and Sylvite, and as Accounting Manager at Cain Express, he brings deep expertise in financial reporting, tax compliance, and business advisory.

Through Mann Accounting and Tax, he provides personalized bookkeeping and tax services to individuals and small businesses — combining rigorous professional standards with a client-first approach.
